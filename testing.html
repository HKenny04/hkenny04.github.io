The Gym

I workout 4 to 6 times a week, depending on how busy I am that week. I believe weight lifting trains me to be more self-disciplined. I have a certain set of rules that I created and follow, such as limiting how much sugar I consume a day and when and what to eat. Self-discipline is a big part of a healthy lifestyle. Lifting also helps me release stress when I feel pressured by school work. Working out is my form of physical and emotional therapy.
