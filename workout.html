My Workout Routine

Personal Stats

I started working out in November 2017

Weight: 145 pounds

Height: 5'8''

Bench Max: 175 pounds

Squat Max: ~225 pounds (haven't attempted a PR in a while so that's an estimate)

Deadlift Max: ~245 pounds (haven't attempted a PR in a while so that's an estimate)

Monday

Chest and Triceps

Flat Bench Press - 8 sets (start with the bar, then gradually go up to max, then gradually down to a plate)

Incline Bench Press - 4 sets

Decline Dumbbell Press - 4 sets

Pec Fly - 4 sets

Lower Chest Cable Fly - 4 sets

Upper Chest Cable Fly - 4 sets

Dips (Dip Machine if you can't do body weight) - 4 sets

Tricep Pushdown - 6 sets

Tricep Overhead Extension with Dumbbell - 4 sets

Kickbacks - 4 sets

Tuesday

Rest Day

Just Chill and rest from Monday. You should be really sore.

Wednesday

Back and Biceps

Thursday

Chest and Triceps

Friday

Legs and Lower Back

Squats - 8 sets (start with the bar, then slowly go up to max, then slowly down to a plate)

Deadlifts - 8 sets (start with a plate, then slowly go up to max, then slowly down to a plate)

Calf Extensions - 6 sets

Hamstring Curls - 6 sets

Quad Extensions - 4 sets (do more if you don't feel sore in your legs after squats C:)

Saturday

Rest Day

You should be sore too. The rest days (twice a week) comes after what I personally think is the most rigorous workout day.

Sunday

Shoulders, Abs and Fat Burner

Overhead Barbell Press - 4 sets

Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 4 sets

Arnold Dumbbell Press - 4 sets

Upright Barbell Row - 4 sets

Side Lateral Raises - 4 sets (dumbbells or cables)

Front Raises - 4 sets (dumbbells, cables, or barbell)

Hanging Leg Raises - 6 sets

Sit-ups and Russian Twists with Weights - 4 sets (basically you grab some weights, perform a sit-up, at the peak do a russian twist, and go back down and repeat)

Crunches - 4 sets

Torso Rotations(machine) - 4 sets

Wood Choppers - 4 sets each side

Rope Crunch - 4 sets

Burpees - 5 sets of 25 each

Mountain Climbers - 5 sets of 50 each

NOTE: I usually grab a cable station (with 2 cables) and do hanging leg raises, rope crunch and wood choppers together.
